­ƒÄ« HOW TO PLAY
--------------
Pitch-by-Pitch Mode (for basement whiffle ball):
1. Pitcher throws from machine
2. Batter swings (or doesn't)
3. Report what happened:
   - Contact made? ÔåÆ Select type (grounder/line drive/pop up) + quality (hard/soft)
   - No contact? ÔåÆ Select swing & miss / no swing / foul ball
4. App calculates realistic outcome
5. Runners advance automatically
6. 3 outs = half inning complete
7. Play your chosen number of innings!

Manual Controls Mode:
- Click "Show Advanced Controls"
- Directly input singles, doubles, walks, strikes, outs, etc.
- Perfect for manual scorekeeping
