📋 Game Rules

⚾ Hit Determination

Determination of type of hit is on your honor.

🚫 No Swing Rule

If you aren't ready to swing, you MUST press "No Swing".

🏏 Bat Requirements

• You must use a skinny bat for one inning (if you have one)

• You can use a fat whiffle bat for one inning (if you have one)

• You must use a real bat for one inning

⚠️ Complaining Penalty

Any complaining results in:

• Simulate Batter button press against the complaining batter, OR

• A walk against the complaining scorekeeper

👥 Player Roles

One player will bat and one player will scorekeep and control the pitching machine.
